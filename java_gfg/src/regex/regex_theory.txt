Regular Expression:
A group of strings according to a pattern
eg: mobile no should contain 10 digits (indian).

Applications:
. Validation Frameworks
. Pattern Searching (Ctrl+F)
. Lexical Analysis in compiler design
. TCP/IP,UDP protocols

Pattern:
. compiled version of regular expression
. equivalent java object of regular expression

Matcher:
. to match the given regex/pattern in the target string.
. can be created using matcher method of Pattern class.
. important methods: find(),start(),end(),group()
